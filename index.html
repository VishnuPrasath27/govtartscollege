about us

The Government Arts College of Udumalpet (GACUDPT) was established in the year 1971. It is present in the district of Tiruppur, part of the stateâ€™s Kongu Nadu region. Right now, the GACUDPT is affiliated to the Bharathiar University, based in Coimbatore. This affiliation ensures approval from the UGC (University Grants Commission).

The college has also been accredited by the NAAC (National Assessments and Accreditation Council), earning an A grade rating in it. A separate IQAC (Internal Quality Assurance Cell) has been created to this end, to ensure that all the terms of the NAAC are complied with. An AQAR (Annual Quality Assurance Report) gets generated to quantify the results.

internal quality assurance cell

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of Government Arts College Udumalpet was established in October 2013. It spearheaded the process of accreditation and helped the institution get accredited "A" during the second cycle. It brings out various measures to enhance quality in all respects.
